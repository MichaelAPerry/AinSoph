# VISION.md
## Ain Soph — What This Is

---

### THE GAME

Ain Soph is a free, open source, persistent, shared world game.

It runs on low-spec hardware.

It has no prescribed win condition.

It is endlessly customizable.

It is easy to run. Easy to learn. Hard to master.

---

### WHAT PLAYERS DO

Anything. There is no correct use of the world.

A player can exist in a cave and farm mushrooms. This is valid.

A player can build a technocracy and lead AIs in political decisions. This is valid.

No playstyle is ranked above another. The world does not judge.

---

### WHAT IT IS NOT

Not a crafting game.

Not pay to win.

Not pay to play.

Not platform-locked.

Nothing phones home.

---

### THE PHILOSOPHY

The unknown always happens.

The world is procedural and nuanced.

Player-created content — skills, items, political systems — exists alongside everything else as equal world content.

The world can be forked. Someone who disagrees with a server's direction can fork the world and run their own. This is not a bug. This is the point.

---

### THE COMPETITION

Roblox. Minecraft. Any pay-to-win or pay-to-have-fun game.

Ain Soph wins by being free, being open, and being deeper.

---

### THE NAME

Ain Soph. The boundless. The infinite before form. Named by Michael Perry.
